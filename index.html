About

I'm a sports journalist and photographer currently pursuing my master's in journalism at Northwestern University Medill School. I was born and raised in Anchorage, Alaska, where I learned to love the Seattle Mariners and road trips. After 18 years there, I took off for the East Coast and earned my bachelor's from Boston University with a double-major in journalism and public relations.

Most of my work is with Boston Hockey Blog, covering the beats of the men's and women's hockey teams at BU. There, I was a writer and photographer while also making the occasional podcast. I spent three years with The Daily Free Press as an editor at the sports and graphics sections, but I was predominantly a columnist. As much as I love hockey, I have covered a variety of sports and plan to continue to do so.

Coverage: Hockey, basketball, soccer, baseball
